Second topic I’d like to have your view about this architecture paradigm using Infrastructure as a service or platform as a service. What are your considerations about them in your project ? I came from companies that used bare metal servers that lived better than ourselves in a cool datacenter where we managed everything. Nowadays in the AWS cloud we still prefer using virtual servers. We can easily support them and have the flexibility to adapt them to our environment needs. I don’t need the painful process of opening AWS support tickets. My partners consider it costly and risky to go to a totally managed cloud solution. So, what are your trade off opinions ?
